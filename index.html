Mattia Martini

I am a Postdoc researcher at the Laboratoire J.A. Dieudonné, Université Côte d'Azur. I am part of the ERC project Elisa led by Prof. François Delarue.

I obtained a PhD in Mathematical Sciences at the Università degli Studi di Milano, under the supervision of Prof. Marco Fuhrman.

My research focuses on stochastic analysis, optimal control and partial differential equations. My main interests concern mean field control, mean field games, stochastic filtering, and their connections with PDEs on spaces of measures.
